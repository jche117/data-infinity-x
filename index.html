The opportunity

Open-weight models changed the game

Frontier-grade capability is no longer locked behind a vendor API. Organisations can now run strong models where their data already lives — and treat AI as a durable asset, not a recurring consumption risk.

Data stays strategic

Enterprise data is not consumed by major AI providers. You retain IP, competitive insight, and regulatory clarity — without feeding your advantage into someone else’s platform.

Materially lower cost

Localised models typically cost far less than routing high volume through frontier APIs. Predictable infrastructure spend replaces open-ended token bills as usage grows.

Runs where you need it

On-premises racks, private cloud, or hybrid — designed for latency, compliance, air-gapped environments, and integration with systems that never leave your network.
